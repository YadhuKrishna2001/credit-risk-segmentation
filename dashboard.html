Risk tier segmentation

150,000 customers split into 5 equal-size tiers by model-predicted default probability. Default rate climbs cleanly from tier to tier — confirms the segmentation actually separates risk.

Top risk drivers

Standardized logistic regression coefficients — larger magnitude = stronger effect on default probability.

Default rate by age band

Younger customers carry substantially more risk — a 5x gap between the riskiest and safest age bands.

Default rate by 90+ days late history

The single strongest predictor: customers with 6+ instances of 90-day-late payments default 14x more than those with none.
